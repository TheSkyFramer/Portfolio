    Websites are often described as 'rooms.' Places that motivate interlocution. They are places we dump parts of ourselves so that when we are unable to meet in person, we are nevertheless able to interact and meet these representations of who we are. This is the beauty of the digital age. Our websites reflect us just as our bedrooms do. Make sure to treat them with care.

    These days, I am pretty poor at communication. So in place of me, this website tells you what I love by showing you. My artistic pursuits displayed here consist of my semester-long efforts, namely my completed Communications Lab projects, photography, and typography. This was the most difficult semester I've ever had. But this website contains evidence that is was somehow all worth it. The way your childhood bedroom is proof of your growth. That's why I also included pictures of my brother, who is in college, actually happy, and in a band. He gives me strength.

    I happen to share a birthday with Leonard Cohen. He has a quote that goes: "If your life is burning well, poetry is just the ash." And I guess this website is the ash.
